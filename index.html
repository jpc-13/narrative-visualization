Wealth and Health Across the World

An interactive slideshow exploring how wealth relates to well-being

This chart shows the relationship between GDP per capita and life expectancy.

Hover over data points to see the exact life expectancy, GDP per capita, and country.

We can see that higher GDP (Gross Domestic Product) tends to correlate with longer life expectancy. The top 20 countries all have life expectancies exceeding 75 years. Also, all countries with life expectancies less than 60 years have GDP per capita less than $10,000.
